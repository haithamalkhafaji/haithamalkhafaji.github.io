About Me

I am currently an incoming student at the University of Washington, where I will be pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Informatics.

I have always had the drive to listen to new music throughout pretty much all of high school and on. I've since established many different accounts on various social platforms such as TikTok and albumoftheyear.org throughout the years, but they were not able to fulfill a void that was left in the creative aspect of content creation.

This website helps address that void by colliding the world of UX Design, Technology, and my personal passion for analytical music documentation. 

I hope you enjoy the content that I publish here. If you have any inquiries, please feel free to reach out at alkhafajihaitham0@gmail.com. I'd love to hear from you :)
